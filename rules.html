Server Rules

General Rules

  1. Respect all players and staff members.
  2. No cheating or hacking of any kind.

Gameplay Rules

  1. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  2. No excessive use of offensive language or behavior.
  3. Report any issues or rule violations to staff members.

PvP Rules

  1. No spawn camping or killing players who are not participating in PvP.
  2. Respect PvP rules set by staff members.

These rules are subject to change at any time. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for breaking them.
